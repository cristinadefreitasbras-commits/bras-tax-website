Meet Cristina

Tax Director • International Tax Advisor • Board Leader

Cristina Halaby-de Freitas Brás is a Tax Director with more than 20 years of experience advising individuals, entrepreneurs and businesses on national and international tax matters throughout the Dutch Caribbean region.

Her expertise spans tax advisory, compliance and strategic planning, with extensive experience in corporate reorganizations, cross-border transactions, estate planning, business succession and expatriate tax services.

Cristina serves as Chairman of both the Curaçao International Financial Center (CIFC) and the Curaçao International Financial Services Association (CIFA).

She is recognized for her commitment to integrity, innovation and collaboration, making her a trusted advisor to clients, colleagues and policymakers alike.

Final Profit Tax Returns 2025

Deadline: 30 November 2026
